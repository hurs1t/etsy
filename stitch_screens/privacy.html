1. Introduction

Welcome to the Legal Center. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Our services are designed to help Etsy sellers optimize their shops. This requires specific data interactions that we want to be completely transparent about.

2. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, connect your Etsy shop, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Shop metadata (title, description, listing details)
  • Usage data and performance metrics
api

3. Etsy API Integration

Our application integrates with the Etsy API to provide its core features. Here is exactly how we handle your Etsy shop data:

Read Access

We analyze your listings to provide SEO suggestions and performance tracking.

Write Access

Only granted when you explicitly authorize listing updates or title optimizations.

Data Storage

We do not store your shop password. All access is managed via secure OAuth tokens.

Revocation

You can revoke our API access at any time through your Etsy account settings.
